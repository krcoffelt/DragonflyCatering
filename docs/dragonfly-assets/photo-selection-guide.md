## Avoid For Now

- Do not invent image coverage for bartending, weddings/showers, event space, or community pages. Those folders are intentionally empty until stronger client-approved assets are added.
- Treat the corporate image as a food/catering placeholder, not proof of an office catering setup.
- Do not use unclear event photos with recognizable guests unless the client confirms rights.
- Do not use any Buchanan Mansion imagery as current venue positioning unless the page is explicitly historical or portfolio-based.

## Cropping Notes

- Food/process images can tolerate tighter crops and text overlays.
- Chef/action images work best as supporting hero or about/private-chef visuals because the subject is recognizable and human.
- Vertical event detail images work best in galleries, service sections, and mobile layouts; use the provided 16:9 hero crop for desktop hero needs.
- Review-only guest images should stay out of public layouts until approved in writing.
